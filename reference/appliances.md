# Appliances and Equipment

## Kitchen Context
UK home kitchen with a mix of Indian and Western cooking styles.

---

## Major Appliances

### AEG Oven
- Modes available:
  - Full Grill
  - Fan + Grill (Turbo Grill)
  - Convection bake
- Preferred usage:
  - Grilling paneer and chicken
  - Traybakes
  - Gratins
- Notes:
  - Door closed during grilling
  - Fan + grill preferred for even cooking

---

### AEG Hob
- Used for:
  - Curries
  - Stir-fries
  - Tadka
- Supports high heat cooking

---

### Crockpot / Slow Cooker
- Used for:
  - Nihari
  - Long braises
- Notes:
  - Final thickening often better on hob
  - Low setting preferred for meat dishes
