# Chicken Pesto Pasta

## Summary
Creamy pesto pasta with chicken, peas, and spinach.  
Balanced, lightly rich, and designed to coat the pasta rather than sit as a heavy sauce.

---

## Ingredients

- Chicken thigh – 250 g, diced
- Pasta – 180–200 g
- Green peas – ½ cup (frozen)
- Spinach – 1–2 handfuls
- Olive oil – 1½ tbsp
- Creme fraiche – 75 g
- Basil pesto – 2–3 tbsp
- Chicken stock pot – 1
- Parmesan – for serving
- Salt – minimal (see notes)
- Black pepper – to taste

---

## Method

### 1. Cook pasta
- Boil pasta in well-salted water until **al dente**
- Reserve **½ cup pasta water**
- Drain and set aside

---

### 2. Cook chicken
- Heat olive oil in a wide pan on medium-high
- Add chicken, season lightly with salt and pepper
- Cook ~7–8 minutes until lightly golden and just cooked

---

### 3. Add peas
- Add peas directly from frozen
- Cook 2 minutes

---

### 4. Build sauce
- Reduce heat to medium-low
- Add pesto, creme fraiche, stock pot
- Add 2–3 tbsp pasta water
- Stir until smooth

---

### 5. Add spinach
- Add spinach and cook until just wilted

---

### 6. Combine pasta
- Add pasta
- Toss continuously
- Add pasta water gradually until sauce is glossy and coating

---

### 7. Finish
- Turn off heat
- Add black pepper
- Taste before adding salt

---

## Notes

- Salt is controlled by stock + pesto + parmesan — adjust only at the end
- Pasta water is essential for emulsification
- Sauce should coat, not pool

---

## Serving Suggestions
- Parmesan
- Black pepper
- Olive oil drizzle

---

## Guest Upgrade

Small changes to elevate the dish without changing its core:

- Deglaze chicken with **2–3 tbsp white wine** after searing; cook off alcohol before adding sauce
- Add **1 extra clove garlic** when cooking chicken
- Finish with **toasted pine nuts or walnuts** for texture
- Add **fresh basil leaves** at the end (not cooked)
- Replace half the creme fraiche with **double cream** for a richer finish
