# Poha

## Summary
Light Karnataka-style poha with coconut, peanuts, and gentle seasoning.  
Designed to be soft, fluffy, and mildly sweet-tangy.

---

## Ingredients

- Thick poha – 1 cup
- Onion – 1 small, chopped
- Peanuts – 2 tbsp
- Green chilli – 1
- Turmeric – ¼ tsp
- Grated coconut – 2–3 tbsp
- Oil – 1½ tbsp
- Lemon juice – 1 tsp
- Salt – to taste
- Sugar – pinch (optional)

---

## Method

### 1. Prep poha
- Rinse briefly (10 seconds)
- Drain and rest 5 minutes
- Add salt + sugar

**Cue:** soft but not mushy

---

### 2. Fry peanuts
- Heat oil
- Fry peanuts until golden
- Remove

---

### 3. Build base
- Cook onion + chilli until soft
- Add turmeric

---

### 4. Add poha
- Add poha and peanuts
- Mix gently
- Sprinkle 1–2 tbsp water
- Cover and steam 2–3 minutes

---

### 5. Finish
- Turn off heat
- Add coconut + lemon
- Mix gently

---

## Notes

### Texture
- Must remain fluffy and light
- Never wet or sticky

### Coconut
- Always added at end for freshness

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With chai or curd
