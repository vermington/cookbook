# Curd Rice

## Summary
Cooling rice dish with yogurt and grapes for mild sweetness.

---

## Ingredients

- Cooked rice – 1 cup
- Yogurt – 1½ cups
- Milk – 2 tbsp
- Grapes – ¼ cup, halved
- Salt – to taste

### Tempering
- Oil – 1 tsp
- Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
- Urad dal – ½ tsp
- Curry leaves – 8
- Ginger – ½ tsp
- Hing – pinch

---

## Method

1. Mash rice lightly
2. Mix with yogurt, milk, salt
3. Add grapes

### Tempering
4. Heat oil, add mustard, dal, curry leaves, ginger, hing
5. Pour over rice

---

## Notes
- Serve at cool room temp
- Do not refrigerate before serving

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With pickle
