# Chicken Curry

## Summary
Spicy, aromatic Chettinad-style chicken curry built on freshly roasted masala with black pepper and fennel.

---

## Ingredients

- Chicken – 1 kg (bone-in preferred)
- Onion – 2, finely sliced
- Tomato – 1, chopped
- Curry leaves – 12–15
- Oil (preferably sesame) – 3 tbsp
- Salt – to taste
- Water – 2 cups

### Masala paste
- Grated coconut – ¼ cup
- Dry red chillies – 5–6
- Coriander seeds – 2 tbsp
- Fennel seeds – 1 tsp
- Cumin seeds – ½ tsp
- Black pepper – 1 tsp
- Cloves – 3
- Cinnamon – 1 inch

---

## Method

### 1. Prepare masala
- Dry roast spices and coconut separately
- Grind to smooth paste with water

---

### 2. Build base
- Heat oil
- Add curry leaves
- Add onions → cook until golden
- Add tomato → cook until soft

---

### 3. Add masala
- Add ground paste
- Cook 3–5 minutes until aromatic

---

### 4. Cook chicken
- Add chicken + salt
- Mix well
- Add water
- Cover and simmer 30–35 minutes

---

### 5. Finish
- Simmer uncovered if needed to thicken

---

## Notes
- Pepper is key — do not reduce too much
- Should be thick, not gravy-heavy

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With dosa or rice

---

## Guest Upgrade
- Add shallots instead of onion
- Finish with coconut oil drizzle
