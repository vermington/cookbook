# Coconut Chutney

## Summary
Sharp, fresh coconut chutney with clean finish, typical of Bangalore darshinis.

---

## Ingredients

- Coconut – 1 cup
- Roasted chana dal – 2 tbsp
- Green chilli – 1–2
- Ginger – small piece
- Salt – to taste
- Water – to adjust

### Tempering
- Oil – 1 tsp
- Mustard seeds – ½ tsp
- Urad dal – ½ tsp
- Curry leaves – 8
- Hing – pinch

---

## Method

1. Grind coconut, dal, chilli, ginger, salt with water
2. Adjust to slightly loose consistency

### Tempering
3. Heat oil
4. Add mustard, urad dal, curry leaves, hing
5. Pour over chutney

---

## Notes
- Should not be thick paste
- Balance chilli vs coconut

---

## Serving Suggestions
- Idli, dosa

---
