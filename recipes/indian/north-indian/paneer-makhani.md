# Paneer Makhani

## Summary
Tomato-forward butter-based paneer curry.  
Balanced, smooth, lightly sweet, not overly creamy.

---

## Ingredients

- [[paneer]] – 250 g, cubed
- [[tomato|Tomatoes]] – 700–800 g, chopped
- Cashews – 15, soaked
- Butter – 3 tbsp
- Cream – ¼ cup

- [[ginger|Ginger]] – 1 inch
- [[garlic|Garlic]] – 6 cloves
- Kashmiri chilli powder – 1½ tsp
- Sugar – 1 tsp
- Kasoori methi – 1 tsp
- Garam masala – ½ tsp
- Salt – to taste
- Water – 1–1½ cups

---

## Method

### 1. Cook base
- Add tomatoes, cashews, ginger, garlic, chilli powder, salt, water
- Simmer 10–12 min until soft

---

### 2. Blend
- Blend smooth
- Strain for silky texture

---

### 3. Build gravy
- Heat butter
- Add puree
- Simmer 8–10 min until slightly thick and glossy

---

### 4. Finish
- Add paneer
- Simmer 3–5 min
- Add cream, kasoori methi, garam masala, sugar

---

### 5. Rest
- Rest 5 minutes before serving

---

## Notes

- No turmeric — keeps colour correct
- Tomato is dominant, not cream
- Paneer should not be overcooked

---

## Serving Suggestions
- Naan or rice

---

## Guest Upgrade

- Add 2 tbsp cream + 1 tbsp butter at end for richness
- Add a few drops kewra water
- Light charcoal smoke (dhungar) before serving
