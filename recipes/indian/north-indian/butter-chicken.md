# Butter Chicken

## Summary
Guest-style butter chicken adapted from the recommended YouTube description.  
This version keeps the two-stage marinade, smoked cashews, and makhani finish, but writes the process for a practical UK home kitchen.

---

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- [[chicken|Chicken leg pieces or boneless thigh pieces]] - 1 kg

### First marinade
- Salt - to taste
- Degi mirch or Kashmiri chilli powder - 1 heaped tsp
- [[ginger|Ginger]]-[[garlic]] paste - 1 tbsp
- Lemon juice - 2 tsp

### Second marinade
- Hung curd or thick [[yogurt]] - 1 1/4 cups
- Ginger-garlic paste - 2 heaped tbsp
- Degi mirch or Kashmiri chilli powder - 2 tbsp
- Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
- Garam masala - 1 tsp
- Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
- Mustard oil - 1 heaped tbsp
- Salt - to taste
- Roasted gram flour - 1 tbsp

### Smoked cashews
- Cashews - 1/2 cup
- Water - as needed
- Ghee - 1 tsp
- Coal - 1 small piece, optional for smoking

### Gravy
- Ghee - 2-3 heaped tbsp
- Green cardamom - 10-12
- Cloves - 4-5
- Cinnamon - 1 inch
- Bay leaves - 2
- [[onion|Onions]] - 6 large, quartered
- Ginger - 1 inch, peeled and roughly chopped
- Green chilli - 1, mild, slit or sliced
- Garlic - 12-15 cloves
- [[tomato|Tomatoes]] - 10-12 large, quartered
- Coriander stems with roots - 2 tbsp, roughly chopped, optional
- Salt - to taste
- Degi mirch or Kashmiri chilli powder - 2 tbsp
- Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
- Sugar - 1/2 tbsp
- Water - 2-3 cups

### Finish
- Butter - 1/3 cup, cubed
- Degi mirch or Kashmiri chilli powder - 1 tsp
- Kasoori methi - 1/2 tsp, crushed
- Prepared gravy
- Cooked chicken
- Water - 1/2 cup, as needed
- Fresh cream - 1/4 cup
- Kasoori methi - 1 tbsp, crushed
- Salt - to taste
- Lemon juice - 2 tsp
- Kewra water - 1 drop, optional

### Garnish
- Fresh cream - optional
- Kasoori methi - pinch
- Coriander sprig - optional

---

## Method

### 1. First marinade
- Mix chicken with salt, degi mirch, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice.
- Rest 20-30 minutes while preparing the second marinade.

---

### 2. Second marinade
- Mix hung curd, ginger-garlic paste, degi mirch, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, mustard oil, and salt.
- Add the chicken and roasted gram flour.
- Coat well and marinate at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

**Cue:** marinade should cling to the chicken, not run off.

---

### 3. Cook and smoke cashews
- Simmer cashews in water for 8-10 minutes until softened.
- Drain and transfer to a small bowl.
- Mix with 1 tsp ghee.
- Optional: place a small steel bowl with hot coal inside the cashew bowl, add a few drops of ghee to the coal, cover, and smoke briefly.

---

### 4. Build gravy base
- Heat ghee in a large pan on medium.
- Add cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and bay leaves.
- Let them sizzle until aromatic.
- Add onions, ginger, and green chilli.
- Cook until onions turn translucent.
- Add garlic, tomatoes, coriander stems, and salt.
- Cook 5-6 minutes on medium until tomatoes begin to soften.
- Add degi mirch, coriander powder, and sugar.
- Cook 1 minute.
- Add 2 cups water and the cashews.
- Simmer until tomatoes are fully mushy.

---

### 5. Blend gravy
- Remove whole bay leaves and any large cinnamon piece if needed.
- Blend the gravy until smooth.
- Strain if a very smooth restaurant texture is wanted.

**Cue:** gravy should be smooth, orange-red, and thick but flowing.

---

### 6. Oven-grill chicken
- Heat the AEG oven to 220°C fan + grill mode.
- Set the marinated chicken on a rack over a lined tray, leaving space between pieces.
- Brush or dot lightly with 1 tbsp ghee.
- Place on the second rack from the top and grill for 10 minutes.
- Move to the top rack and grill 3-5 minutes more for light charring.
- Remove and rest while finishing the gravy.

**Cue:** chicken should be charred at the edges and partly cooked through, but not fully dried out. It will finish in the gravy.

---

### 7. Finish butter chicken
- Heat butter in a pan on medium-low.
- Add 1 tsp degi mirch and 1/2 tsp kasoori methi.
- Cook briefly until the butter turns red and aromatic.
- Add prepared gravy and cooked chicken.
- Add 1/2 cup water if needed.
- Simmer 10-12 minutes on medium-low until the chicken is cooked through and the gravy coats it well.
- Finish with cream, 1 tbsp kasoori methi, salt, lemon juice, and optional kewra water.

---

## Notes
- This is intentionally richer and more restaurant-style than the house paneer makhani.
- The gravy uses a large amount of onion and tomato; test once before reducing.
- Kewra is powerful. Use only 1 drop or skip.
- If using boneless thigh, watch cooking time more closely than bone-in leg pieces.
- Oven-grilled chicken should be slightly under-finished before it goes into the gravy; the final 10-12 minute simmer completes cooking.

## Variants
- Lighter version: reduce butter to 3 tbsp and cream to 2 tbsp after testing.
- No-smoke version: skip the coal-smoked cashews.
- Grill-pan version: cook the marinated chicken in 1 tbsp ghee on medium-high for 5-6 minutes per side until browned and half-cooked, then finish in the gravy.
