# Panchratni Dal

## Summary
Mixed lentil dish with balanced texture and flavour. Should be creamy but not muddy.

---

## Ingredients

- Toor dal – ¼ cup
- Moong dal – ¼ cup
- Chana dal – ¼ cup
- Masoor dal – ¼ cup
- Urad dal – 2 tbsp

- Onion – 1, chopped
- Tomato – 1, chopped
- Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
- Oil/ghee – 2 tbsp
- Salt – to taste
- Water – 3 cups

### Spices
- Turmeric – ½ tsp
- Chilli powder – 1 tsp
- Coriander powder – 2 tsp
- Garam masala – ½ tsp

---

## Method

### 1. Cook dal
- Pressure cook all dals with turmeric until soft
- Mash lightly

---

### 2. Make masala
- Heat oil
- Cook onion until golden
- Add ginger-garlic
- Add tomato and cook down

---

### 3. Combine
- Add dal
- Add water to adjust consistency
- Simmer 10–15 min

---

### 4. Finish
- Add garam masala
- Optional ghee finish

---

## Notes
- Maintain some texture — do not overblend
- Balance thickness carefully

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With rice or roti

---

## Guest Upgrade
- Add tadka with ghee, garlic, and cumin
