# Masala Veg Pulao

## Summary
Lightly spiced vegetable rice using sona masoori.  
Aromatic but not heavy, designed as a one-dish meal.

---

## Ingredients

- Sona masoori rice – 2 cups (soaked)
- Mixed vegetables – 1½ cups (carrot, beans, peas, broccoli)
- Onion – 1 large, sliced
- Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
- Green chilli – 2

- Ghee/oil – 3 tbsp
- Water – 3½ cups
- Salt – to taste

### Whole spices
- Bay leaf – 1
- Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
- Cloves – 3
- Cardamom – 2
- Cinnamon – 1 inch

### Powder spices
- Turmeric – ½ tsp
- Chilli powder – 1 tsp
- Coriander powder – 2 tsp
- Garam masala – ½ tsp

---

## Method

### 1. Build base
- Heat ghee/oil
- Add whole spices
- Add onion → cook until golden
- Add ginger-garlic and chilli

---

### 2. Add vegetables
- Add harder veg first (carrot, beans)
- Add softer veg later (peas, broccoli)
- Cook briefly

---

### 3. Add spices
- Add powdered spices
- Stir with small water splash

---

### 4. Cook rice
- Add soaked rice
- Add water + salt
- Bring to boil, cover, cook low heat ~15 min

---

### 5. Finish
- Add garam masala
- Rest 5 minutes before fluffing

---

## Notes

- Sona masoori gives softer texture than basmati
- Keep moisture controlled to avoid mushiness
- Broccoli added late to avoid overcooking

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With raita

---

## Guest Upgrade

- Add fried onions on top
- Add a handful of mint leaves during cooking
- Finish with a small knob of butter
