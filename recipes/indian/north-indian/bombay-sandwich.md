# Bombay Sandwich

## Summary
Layered vegetable sandwich with chutney, spices, and soft bread.

---

## Ingredients

- Bread – 6 slices
- Boiled potato – 2, sliced
- Cucumber – sliced
- Tomato – sliced
- Onion – sliced
- Butter – for spreading

### Chutney
- Coriander
- Mint
- Green chilli
- Lemon juice
- Salt

---

## Method

1. Spread butter + chutney on bread
2. Layer potato, cucumber, tomato, onion
3. Sprinkle chaat masala
4. Close and cut

---

## Notes
- Thin slicing is key
- Keep layers balanced

---

## Serving Suggestions
- With chai
