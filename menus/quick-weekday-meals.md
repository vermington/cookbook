# Quick Weekday Meals

## Goal
Meals that:
- take under 30 minutes
- require minimal prep
- are balanced and filling

---

## Options

### Indo-Chinese
- Egg + veg fried rice
- Chicken fried rice

### Indian
- Poha
- Paneer capsicum (dry)
- Simple dal + rice

### Western / Fusion
- Miso peanut noodles
- Mediterranean chicken traybake

---

## Notes
- Prefer one-dish meals
- Avoid multi-curry spreads on weekdays
