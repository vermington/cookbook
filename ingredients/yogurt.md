# Yogurt
Fermented dairy that adds tang and body; thickness varies from set yogurt to hung curd.

## Common uses
- Marinades for kebabs and rolls
- Curries and kadhi
- Raita and dips

## Substitutions
- Greek yogurt for thicker body; thin with water if needed
- Plant-based yogurt for dairy-free versions (watch sweetness/starch)

## Notes
- Add to hot dishes off heat or on low to prevent splitting; whisk smooth before mixing.
