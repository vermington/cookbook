# Onion
Savory-sweet base for many curries, stir-fries, and salads; texture changes define dish style.

## Common uses
- Bhuna bases for North Indian curries
- Quick sautés for sabzis and Indo-Chinese dishes
- Fresh slices for rolls and chaats

## Substitutions
- Shallots for milder flavour
- Leeks for gentle sweetness in Western dishes

## Notes
- For bhuna, cook to deep golden but not bitter; for crunch (e.g., kathi rolls) keep slices raw or lightly pickled.
