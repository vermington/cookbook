# Tomato
Sweet-acidic base ingredient for gravies, sauces, and chutneys; ripe tomatoes give balance without extra sugar.

## Common uses
- North Indian gravies, makhani-style bases
- Stir-fry splashes or quick sauces

## Substitutions
- Tinned tomatoes when good fresh tomatoes are unavailable (adjust sweetness)
- Tomato puree for colour/intensity in small amounts

## Notes
- Cook down until raw smell disappears and fat separates for deeper flavour; avoid dominating dishes that should be onion-forward.
