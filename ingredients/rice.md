# Rice
Staple grain; choice of variety sets texture—sona masoori for everyday South Indian meals, basmati for pulao or biryani.

## Common uses
- Steamed accompaniment
- Fried rice and pulao
- Idli/dosa batters when combined with dals

## Substitutions
- Short-grain rice for stickier texture in some East Asian dishes
- Quinoa or millets if avoiding rice (flavour and timing differ)

## Notes
- For fried rice, use day-old or well-cooled rice so grains stay separate; rinse basmati until water runs clear for fluffier results.
