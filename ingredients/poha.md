# Poha
Flattened rice flakes used for quick breakfasts and snacks; texture ranges from thin to thick.

## Common uses
- Breakfast poha with onions, peanuts, and spices
- Crisping in chivda or snack mixes

## Substitutions
- Thick poha ↔ thin poha depending on dish (adjust soak/rinse time)
- Rice flakes from other brands; avoid over-soaking to prevent mush

## Notes
- Rinse briefly and rest to hydrate; avoid soaking unless recipe specifies.
