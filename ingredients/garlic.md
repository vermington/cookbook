# Garlic
Savory, sulphurous aromatic that deepens flavour when lightly browned but turns bitter if burnt.

## Common uses
- Bhuna bases, stir-fries, tempering oils
- Chutneys and marinades

## Substitutions
- Garlic powder for dry rubs (not equivalent in curries)
- Asafoetida in small amounts for no-garlic variations

## Notes
- For Indo-Chinese stir-fries, keep it just fragrant and pale; for curries, cook to light golden to remove raw edge.
