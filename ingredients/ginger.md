# Ginger
Fresh, pungent root providing heat and brightness; often paired with garlic in Indian cooking.

## Common uses
- Base aromatics in curries and marinades
- Finishing juliennes for garnish and aroma

## Substitutions
- Galangal in some Southeast Asian dishes (different citrusy profile)
- Ginger powder only for baking or dry rubs; lacks freshness

## Notes
- Add early for rounded flavour; add slivers near the end when you want the freshness to stand out.
