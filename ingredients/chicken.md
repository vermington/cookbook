# Chicken
Versatile protein; curry-cut bone-in pieces for gravies, boneless thighs or breast for quick cooks.

## Common uses
- Curries, kebabs, grills, slow braises
- Stock or broth bases

## Substitutions
- Turkey thigh for similar texture in curries
- Firm paneer or mushrooms for vegetarian swaps (flavour profile changes)

## Notes
- Bone-in pieces give better flavour for slow-cooked dishes; avoid overcooking breast in high-heat dishes.
