# Coconut
Rich, fragrant component used fresh, desiccated, or as milk/cream in many South Indian and coastal dishes.

## Common uses
- Chutneys and masala bases
- Coconut milk for curries and stews
- Desiccated coconut for tempering or sweets

## Substitutions
- Coconut milk powder (reconstituted) when fresh milk unavailable
- Cream reduced with a little water for body (flavour less coconutty)

## Notes
- Freshly ground coconut gives best aroma; simmer coconut milk gently to avoid splitting.
