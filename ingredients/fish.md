# Fish
Lean or fatty depending on species; texture and flavour vary, so choose style-appropriate fish.

## Common uses
- Coastal curries, shallow fries, grills
- Stews with coconut milk

## Substitutions
- White firm fish (cod, haddock) interchangeable in many curries
- Salmon for richer, fattier result (adjust cooking time)

## Notes
- Pat dry before frying to reduce splatter; avoid overcooking delicate fillets.
