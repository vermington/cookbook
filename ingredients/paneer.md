# Paneer
Fresh, mildly tangy cheese used for North and South Indian dishes; keeps shape under heat if handled gently.

## Common uses
- Curries like makhani, kadai, butter masala
- Dry dishes and stir-fries where browning gives texture
- Kebabs and stuffed breads

## Substitutions
- Firm tofu in vegan contexts (texture softer, flavour neutral)
- Halloumi for grilling, but saltier and squeakier

## Notes
- Best results with fresh, soft paneer; brief sear improves structure without toughening.
