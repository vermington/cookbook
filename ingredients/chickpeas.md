# Chickpeas
Nutty pulses used whole, mashed, or milled; available dried or tinned.

## Common uses
- Curries like chole
- Hummus and spreads
- Salads and roasted snacks

## Substitutions
- Tinned chickpeas for speed (rinse to reduce salinity)
- Other beans (cannellini) in a pinch, flavour shifts

## Notes
- Soak dried chickpeas 8–12 hours with salt for even cooking; pressure cook for creamy interiors.
